About Us

We are one of Colorado's largest year-round farmers markets! Over 100 vendors every Saturday sell the freshest fruits, vegetables, meats, lotions, soaps out there! During the summertime, we're thankful to have local artists perform in our Seven Hills Summer Concert series. Food trucks all across Colorado serve food and drinks. All ages are welcomed! We guarantee everyone will find something to enjoy during their visit. Parking is free, parking attendants will help direct visitors. What are you waiting for? We'll see you this Saturday!

Vendors

  • Fresh Produce: vegetables, fruits, herbs, berries
  • Meat, Poultry, Dairy, Beef, Bison, Pork, Chicken, Eggs, Milk, Cheese
  • Baked Goods: Artisan Bread, pies, cakes, cookies, pastries
  • Condiments & Preserves: jams, jellies, relishes, hot sauces, salsa
  • Sweeteners & Sweets: honey, maple syrup, chocolates, candies
  • Flowers & Plants: cut & potted flowers, potted herbs, bedding plants
  • Household items: soaps, lotions, cleaning products, textiles
  • Artisan Wares: pottery, wool, knitted goods, paper, photography
